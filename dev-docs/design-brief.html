Web Guidelines

Recommended Design Direction

Option A — Dark & Earthy

  • Background Dark olive #303010 or near-black #1a1a0a
  • Text Cream #F0F0D0 / Ivory #F0F0E0
  • Accents Copper #C07040 and Gold #E0A050
  • CTA Strava Orange #FC4C02

Immersive, night-ride energy — logo pops on dark backgrounds.

Option B — Light & Vintage

  • Background Cream #F0F0D0 or warm white #F5F5E8
  • Text Dark olive #303010 / Brown #402010
  • Accents Copper highlights, muted sage dividers
  • CTA Strava Orange or Dark Olive buttons

Parchment/vintage poster — bright and welcoming.

Layout Principles

  1. Logo-first hero — the crest is the centerpiece of every layout
  2. Generous whitespace — let the badge breathe with ample padding
  3. Single strong CTA — Strava join link stays the primary action
  4. Expandable sections — about, upcoming rides, photo gallery, member count, route highlights

Usage Notes

  • The source filename has a typo (dirtchucrch vs dirtchurch) — be aware when referencing art assets.
  • The logo uses RGBA transparency — always place on intentional backgrounds, never default white.
  • Strava Orange should be reserved exclusively for Strava-related CTAs to maintain platform association.
  • Dark olive #303010 is the single most defining brand color — use it consistently as the anchor.
